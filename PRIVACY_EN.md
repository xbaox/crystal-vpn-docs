## 1. What we collect

The minimum necessary:

- **Telegram ID** — for identification in the bot;
- **Telegram username** (if set) — stored as part of the payment log and gift records;
- **the name** the Customer entered at registration (can be changed in the bot settings);
- **the chosen interface language**, notification settings and time zone;
- **acceptance marks** for the offer and this Policy (date and time);
- **subscription history** — plan, period, expiry date, issued devices;
- **payment and receipt log** — payment ID, method, amount, date;
- **gift content** — when buying a subscription as a gift, we store the greeting text and a
  reference to the attached image (a Telegram file_id) provided by the Customer. The image
  itself is stored within Telegram's infrastructure; we store only the reference and the text
  and use them solely to show them to the Recipient on gift activation;
- **aggregated traffic** — total bytes per device (for the statistics screen);
- **payout details** — only for Partners of the partner program, to the extent provided by the Partner themselves.

## 2. What we do NOT collect

- **Traffic contents** — we cannot see which sites are visited or what data is transferred.
  Traffic is encrypted from device to server (the AmneziaWG and VLESS + Reality protocols).
- **Connection logs** — the VPN nodes keep no logs of connections or DNS queries:
  who connected, when and from which IP address is not recorded.
- Browsing history, search history, contacts, media files — we do not collect or store.
- **Bank card data** — for card payments it is processed by the **ЮKassa (ЮMoney NBCO LLC)**
  payment service. The bot does not collect, receive or store card details; only the fact of
  payment, order number and amount are available to it.

## 3. Why we need this data

For identification, granting and renewing access, showing statistics and enforcing plan limits,
keeping payment records, generating receipts and handling possible refunds, delivering a gift to
its Recipient, and for accruing bonuses and partner payouts.

## 4. Who we share data with

We do not share data with third parties, except:

- **ЮMoney NBCO LLC** (ЮKassa) — to process a payment; the amount, payment purpose and Telegram ID
  are transferred in service metadata;
- **Telegram** — for Telegram Stars payments (the amount and service order data);
- **@CryptoBot** — for cryptocurrency payments; the asset, amount and service order data, including
  the Telegram ID, are transferred;
- **the Federal Tax Service of Russia («Moy Nalog» service)** — when a receipt is generated; only
  the service name, amount and quantity are transferred, the buyer's data is not included in the receipt;
- **the gift Recipient** — the greeting and image attached to a gift are shown to the person who
  activated the gift code;
- requests from authorized bodies under the law of the hosting country (only what we hold is
  provided; we do not have traffic contents).

The documents website and the post-payment return page are hosted on GitHub Pages and use Google
Fonts — these services technically have access to the IP address of a page visitor when the pages are opened.

## 5. Where and how long data is stored

- Profile, subscriptions and gift records (including greetings) — while the Service exists or until
  a deletion request (clause 6);
- Payment log and receipts — for the entire operating period of the Service, subject to tax-law
  requirements; a deletion request does not apply to them;
- Traffic snapshots — 35 days, then deleted automatically;
- Application technical logs (bot operation events) — a limited volume with rotation; they contain
  no traffic contents;
- Database backups — in encrypted form, for restoring the Service.

## 6. Customer rights

The Customer may request deletion of their profile: the name and profile-related data are
anonymized, while the payment log and receipts are retained as mandatory to keep. An export of the
data we hold about them may also be requested.
