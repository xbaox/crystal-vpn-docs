## 3. Standard Referral Program

**3.1.** Every bot user receives a unique referral link formatted as
`t.me/Crysta1_VPNbot?start=ref_<id>`. The link is permanent and does not
change.

**3.2.** For every invited friend who pays for any tariff for the **first
time** using any payment method available in the bot, the Referrer
receives **14 days** added to their subscription. The bonus value may be
changed; the current value is shown in the bot.

**3.3.** The invited friend receives a **15% discount** on their first
purchase. The discount is one-time, applied automatically at checkout, and
does not apply to renewals. The discount value may be changed; the current
value is shown in the bot.

**3.4.** The following do not count as a payment and do not trigger the
Referrer's bonus:
- activation of a free promo code (e.g., `БАО`, in Cyrillic);
- activation of a gift code by the Recipient (payment means the purchase of the gift made by the
  buyer, not the activation of the code);
- a payment refund.

**3.5.** Bonus days:
- cannot be withdrawn as money or exchanged for other services;
- cannot be transferred to other users;
- if the Referrer has no active subscription at the time of accrual, they
  accumulate in "pending" status and are applied automatically upon the next
  purchase;
- pending days do not expire.

**3.6.** One user — one binding forever. If the invited person entered the
bot before following the Referrer's link, no binding is created.
Self-referral (using one's own link) is prohibited.

**6.1.** Self-referral (using one's own link or promo code). Commissions and

**6.2.** Fraudulent inflation using farm services, fake Telegram accounts, or

**6.3.** Spam promotion methods: mass direct messaging to strangers, posting
in other people's chats without their admins' permission, or using spam bots
in comments.

**6.4.** Misleading advertising. In advertising, the Partner must not:
- promise "bypassing blocks", "censorship circumvention", or "100%
  anonymity" — Crystal VPN is not positioned as an anti-censorship service;
- claim that the Service works "always" / "without interruptions" /
  "guaranteed";
- promise non-existent features (unlimited data, choice of 50+ countries,
  etc.);
- use logos of banned organizations or mislead users about the legal status
  of the Service.

**6.5.** Advertising directed at minors under 13 years old is strictly
prohibited.

**6.6.** Publicly disclosing the individual commission rates of other
Partners (if learned through the Administrator's negligence).

**6.7.** **Mimicking the official brand.** The Partner is strictly forbidden
to present their resources as official representations of the Service.
Therefore:
- **it is prohibited** to use the name "Crystal VPN" (or any similar
  variations) as the name of your Telegram channel, group, bot, or personal
  profile;
- **it is prohibited** to set the official Crystal VPN logo as the avatar of
  your platforms or channels;
- **it is permitted** to use interface screenshots, bot profile snapshots in
  Telegram, and official promo materials solely *within* your promotional
  posts, articles, or videos to demonstrate the product.

The Partner's advertising resource must have its own name, unique identity,
and original content. Violating this requirement is equivalent to misleading
advertising (clause 6.4).

Violation of any point in Section 6 is grounds for partner deactivation.
