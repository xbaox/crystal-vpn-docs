## 2. Standard Referral Program

**2.1.** Every bot user receives a unique referral link formatted as `t.me/CrystalVPN_bot?start=ref_<your_id>`. The link is permanent and does not change.

**2.2.** For every invited friend who pays for any tariff for the **first time** using any available method (Telegram Stars or CryptoBot), the referrer receives **14 days** added to their subscription (this value may be changed — check the bot for the current value).

**2.3.** The invited friend receives a **15% discount** on their first purchase. The discount is one-time and applied automatically when purchasing a tariff. The discount does not apply to renewals.

**2.4.** What **does not count** as a payment and does not trigger the referrer's bonus:
- activation of a free promo code (e.g., `BAO`);
- trial period;
- payment refund.

**2.5.** Bonus days:
- cannot be withdrawn as money or exchanged for other services;
- cannot be transferred to other users;
- if you do not have an active subscription at the time of accrual, they accumulate in "pending" status and are applied automatically upon your next purchase;
- pending days do not expire.

**2.6.** One user = one binding forever. If a friend has already entered the bot before using your link, the binding is not created. Self-referrals are prohibited.

## 3. Partner Program

**3.1.** Partner status is granted **only by the administrator** on an individual basis. You cannot submit an application; the administrator selects partners personally.

**3.2.** Upon receiving the status, the partner is provided with:
- a unique text promo code (e.g., `OLEG15`);
- a partner referral link `t.me/CrystalVPN_bot?start=p_<your_code>`;
- individual commission rates (default: 45% from the first payment, 15% from all subsequent renewals by the same user).

**3.3.** Commission is accrued from **every** payment made by a user bound to the partner, throughout their lifetime in the bot. It is calculated based on the actual amount the user paid (after the 15% discount is applied).

**3.4.** All accrued commissions accumulate in the partner's personal balance in the "💼 Partner Program" section of the bot.

**3.5.** Payouts are executed manually by the administrator upon the partner's request. Before requesting, the partner must fill in "💳 My Payment Details" in the bot's cabinet (bank card / USDT address / other details).

**3.6.** Payout timeframe: up to 7 business days from the moment of request. For large amounts (>50,000 ₽), the administrator may request identity verification.

**3.7.** The minimum amount for a payout request is **not set**. You can request any remaining balance.

**3.8.** The payout currency is agreed upon with the partner (Rubles to a card / USDT TRC20 / TON).

## 4. What is Prohibited

**4.1.** Self-referral (using your own link or promo code) — commission for such actions is automatically annulled, and accounts may be deleted.

**4.2.** Fraudulent inflation using farm services, fake Telegram accounts, or mass user SMS activations — commissions are annulled for the entire period, and partner status is revoked without the right to restore.

**4.3.** Spam advertising methods: mass direct messaging to strangers, posting in other people's chats without admin permission, or using spam bots in comments.

**4.4.** Misleading advertising. The partner must not:
- promise "bypassing government blocks", "censorship circumvention", or "100% anonymity" — Crystal VPN is not positioned as an anti-censorship service;
- claim that the VPN works "always" / "without interruptions" / "guaranteed";
- promise features that do not exist (unlimited data, choice of 50+ countries, etc.);
- use logos of banned organizations or mislead users regarding the legal status of the service.

**4.5.** Advertising to minors (under 13 years old — strictly prohibited).

**4.6.** Publicly disclosing the individual partner commission rates of other partners (if learned through the administrator's negligence).

**4.7.** **Mimicking the official brand.** The partner is strictly forbidden to present their resources as official representations of the service. Therefore:
- **It is prohibited** to use the name "Crystal VPN" (as well as any similar variations) as the name of your Telegram channel, group, bot, or personal profile.
- **It is prohibited** to set the official Crystal VPN logo as the avatar for your platforms or channels.
- **It is permitted** to use interface screenshots, bot profile snapshots in Telegram, and official promo materials solely *within* your promotional posts, articles, or videos to demonstrate the product.

Violation of any point in §4 is grounds for partner deactivation.

Your advertising resource must have its own name, unique identity, and original content. Violating this point is equivalent to misleading advertising (see clause 4.4).

**5.1.** The administrator has the right to deactivate a partner at any time: for violating §4, for inactivity of more than 6 months, or for reasons not disclosed to the partner (e.g., conflicts of interest with other partners).

**5.3.** If a partner considers the deactivation unfair, they can direct message the administrator. The administrator's decision is final.

**5.4.** In the event of a user refund (refund in Telegram Stars or chargeback in CryptoBot), the partner's commission for this payment **is not automatically rolled back**. If the case is clearly fraudulent, the administrator may manually reverse it and notify the partner.
