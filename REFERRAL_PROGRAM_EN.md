## 1. Standard referral — for everyone

**Available to every bot user.** No setup needed — you already have a
personal link.

### How it works in three steps

1. **Open the bot** → tap «🤝 Invite a friend» in the main menu.
2. **Copy your link** — it looks like:
   ```
   t.me/CrystalVPN_bot?start=ref_<your_id>
   ```
3. **Send it to a friend** — DM, chat, story, anywhere.

### What the referrer (you) gets

🎁 **+14 days of free subscription** for every friend who pays for any tariff.

- The bonus is credited **only after the friend's first paid purchase**.
- A promocode (free activation) **doesn't count** as a purchase — no
  bonus from it.
- If you have an **active subscription** — 14 days are added to its
  expiry instantly. You get: *«🎁 Your friend got a subscription! +14
  days»*.
- If you **have no subscription** — 14 days are stored in your personal
  "pending wallet". When you buy a subscription next time — the bonus
  applies on top automatically. Nothing lost.

### What the referral (your friend) gets

🎉 **15% discount on the first purchase.**

- The discount applies automatically — the friend just opens your link
  and sees it right on the tariff card.
- The discount is **one-time** — first purchase only.
- It doesn't apply to renewals (but the partner % continues if you're a
  partner — see §2).

### Rules and limits

- **One binding — forever.** A friend is bound to you on their first
  `/start`. If they later open someone else's link — the original
  binding stays.
- **New users only.** If the friend was already in the bot before you
  sent them the link — no binding.
- **No self-referrals.** If you open your own link — it's ignored.
- **Referral discount doesn't stack with `legacy` discount** (for old
  BAO clients). The maximum of the two is used.
- **No limit on how many friends to invite.** Even 100 — bonuses add up.

### How to share conveniently

The bot has a **«📤 Share link»** button — tap it, Telegram suggests a
chat to share, and the link with text is pre-filled.

## 2. Partner program — for those who promote

**This isn't for everyone.** Partner status is granted only by BAO
(admin) personally. If you want to become a partner — message:
**[@bao_gigastore](https://t.me/bao_gigastore)**.

### Who it's for

- Bloggers, streamers, channel owners with an audience.
- Chat admins promoting the service in their communities.
- Those who actually bring users — not just "give myself a 15% discount".

### What the partner gets

💰 **Money. Real ₽ that BAO transfers manually.**

When your referral pays:
- **First payment:** **45%** of the amount (default; may differ in
  your agreement).
- **All renewals:** **15%** of the amount.

Commissions accumulate on your in-bot balance. When you request a
payout — BAO gets a notification with your details and transfers the
money outside the bot (bank/card/crypto — whatever you agreed on).

### What the referral (your client) gets

The same as in the standard referral: **15% discount on first purchase**.
The 15% client discount and the partner % are **different money**:
- 15% goes to the **client** (lowers their bill).
- The partner % goes to **you** (from the amount the client actually
  paid).

### Two acquisition tools

After receiving partner status, you'll have **two** ways to bind a client:

1. **Deep link:** `t.me/CrystalVPN_bot?start=p_<your_code>`
   *(example: `t.me/CrystalVPN_bot?start=p_OLEG15`)*
2. **Text promocode:** the client enters your code themselves in the
   «🎁 Promocode» screen.

Both methods give the same result: the client is bound to you + gets
15% off. Use whichever is more convenient for the channel: link for
social media, promocode for voice mention in YouTube/podcasts.

### How to become a partner

1. **Message BAO** on Telegram: [@bao_gigastore](https://t.me/bao_gigastore).
2. Briefly say: who you are, where you'll promote, expected audience.
3. BAO decides: add or not. If added — gives you:
   - `partner_code` (your promocode, letters/digits, e.g. `OLEG15`)
   - `percent_first` (% of first payment, usually 45)
   - `percent_renewal` (% of renewals, usually 15)
4. After activation, the bot will show **«💼 Partner program»** in
   the main menu.

### Partner cabinet

In **«💼 Partner program»** you'll see:

- 🔗 **Your link** and 🎟 **promocode** (with quick-copy buttons)
- 📊 **Stats:**
  - Total referrals
  - Active ones
  - Earned **all-time**
  - Payable **right now**
- 👥 **List of referrals**
- 💰 **Payout history**
- 📤 **Request payout**
- 💳 **Payment details** (you fill them yourself — where to send money)

### How to get paid (payout request)

1. In the cabinet → **«💳 Payment details»** → fill in (bank / card /
   crypto-wallet — whatever you prefer).
2. Tap **«📤 Request payout»**.
3. BAO gets a notification with your amount and details.
4. Transfers outside the bot → marks as closed in admin panel → you
   get **«✅ Payout of X ₽ closed»**.

**No minimum for a payout request** — even 500 ₽ or 50 000 ₽. BAO
decides in fact whether to transfer (if the sum is too small, they
might ask you to accumulate — but that's outside the rules, just common
sense).

### Partner deactivation

If BAO removes status (`is_active=0`):
- **New** users via your link/code **aren't bound**.
- **Already bound** ones stop generating commission from new payments.
- **Accumulated balance** stays — BAO will pay it out.

This is insurance against "partner stopped promoting, but already-bound
clients keep generating income forever".

## 3. Payment methods

### ⭐ Telegram Stars (main)

Native Telegram-stars payments. Stars are bought directly in Telegram
(«⭐ Premium and Gifts» in settings) or via exchange bots — e.g.
**[@StarsFreeRuBot](https://t.me/StarsFreeRuBot)** (if direct purchase
isn't available in your country).

**Pros:**
- Instant — 1-2 seconds.
- No registration, no cards.
- Telegram-first — works everywhere TG works.

**Cons:**
- Stars→₽ rate slightly floats.

### 💎 CryptoBot (USDT / TON)

For those paying in crypto. Uses the official service
**[@CryptoBot](https://t.me/CryptoBot)** (a.k.a. `aiocryptopay`).

**How to pay:**
1. On the tariff card → **«💎 Pay with crypto»**.
2. Choose asset: **USDT** or **TON**.
3. A 30-minute invoice opens — go to @CryptoBot and pay.
4. Within 30 seconds after payment — the bot sends **«🎉 Thanks for
   your purchase!»** with devices.

**Pros:**
- Privacy — CryptoBot allows anonymous payments.
- No Stars needed.
- Crypto→₽ rate fixed at the moment the invoice is created.

**Cons:**
- 30-second delay (vs 1-2 for Stars) — this is a polling job that
  asks CryptoBot every 30s.

**If the bot doesn't show «💎 Pay with crypto»** — the feature is
currently off (no CryptoBot token in BAO's config). That's fine, Stars
work in that case.

### Which to choose

| | Stars | CryptoBot |
|---|---|---|
| Speed | instant | ~30 sec |
| Simplicity | very | needs crypto |
| Anonymity | medium | high |
| Available in RU | yes | yes |

**Recommendation for most: Stars.** Crypto — if you're already into it.

## 4. FAQ

### 🤝 About standard referral

**Q: Can I opt out of the referral program?**
A: Yes. If you don't share the link — it doesn't activate on its own.
No "opt-out" needed.

**Q: What if my friend doesn't pay?**
A: Nothing scary. The binding stays in the DB, the bonus doesn't
trigger for you. But if the friend pays **ever** — even a year later —
you'll get +14 days automatically.

**Q: Can a referrer give themselves a discount via their own link?**
A: No. The bot checks: if `referrer_tg_id == your_tg_id` — the link is
ignored. Anti-cheat.

**Q: Does the 15% discount apply to renewals?**
A: No, only to the **first** purchase. Renewals are full price.

**Q: What if I'm expired but the referral paid?**
A: The +14 days bonus is stored in `referral_pending_bonuses`. Next
time you buy any tariff/period — it applies automatically. No expiry
date on the pending bonus.

**Q: And if I NEVER had a subscription, but the friend paid?**
A: Same — the bonus waits in pending. Buy in a year — get it.

**Q: Can I transfer bonus days to someone else?**
A: No, bonuses are tied to your `tg_id`.

### 💼 About partnership

**Q: What if I stop promoting?**
A: Already-bound users keep generating % from renewals **while you're
an active partner**. If BAO removes status — commissions stop.

**Q: Can I apply for partnership myself?**
A: No, the bot doesn't accept applications. Partnership is a personal
agreement between you and BAO. Message directly.

**Q: Can I use my partner code for myself?**
A: No. Same as referral — self-binding forbidden.

**Q: What if I enter wrong payment details?**
A: Nothing scary — BAO will message you to clarify. You can change
details anytime through the cabinet.

**Q: What if the client asks for a refund?**
A: At launch — handled personally (no automatic refunds in the bot).
If refunded, BAO can adjust your balance manually.

**Q: Is there a minimum payout amount?**
A: No hard minimum. But if you have 50 ₽ — BAO will probably ask you
to accumulate up to at least 500-1000.

### 💎 About payment

**Q: If CryptoBot hangs for 5 minutes — what do I do?**
A: On the invoice screen there's a **«🔄 Check payment»** button — tap
it, the bot queries CryptoBot API directly and shows the current
status. If paid — subscription applies within the same 30 seconds
(when polling triggers).

**Q: Can I pay half with Stars and half with crypto?**
A: No. One payment — one method.

**Q: What if I paid but the bot didn't activate my subscription?**
A: Message BAO ([@bao_gigastore](https://t.me/bao_gigastore)) with the
transaction number — they'll check manually. It's a very rare situation
(hasn't happened yet), but the insurance is there.
